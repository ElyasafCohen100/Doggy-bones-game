## ✨ Overview ✨

**Doggy Bones Game** is a cute and fun arcade-style game built with **Processing** 🐶🦴  
You play as a little dog who loves collecting bones — but be careful!  
If you lose all your hearts, it’s game over 💔

The goal is simple: **collect as many bones as possible and reach a high score** 🎯  
Easy to play, fun to master!

---

## 🎮 How to Play 🎮

- ⌨️ Move the dog using the keyboard  
- 🦴 Catch the falling bones  
- ❤️ Avoid losing all your hearts  
- 🏆 Try to beat your high score  

---

## 📁 Project Files 📁

- `MyFirstApp.pde` – main game logic  
- `nitz13D.pde` – helper classes and extra logic  
- `data/` – images, sounds, and fonts
